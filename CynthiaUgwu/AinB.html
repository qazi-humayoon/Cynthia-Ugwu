(REVAMP)

Even though PlugNG was already an established product,it needed a
fresh appearance to provide its intended benefits and assist users in
easily navigating the app.

(USABILITY)

An unsequenced arrangement and flow on the site made it difficult for
users to navigate, which posed a risk of increased downtime for users on
the product.

(ACCESSBILITY)

One of the crucial aspects of the product was to onboard artists for
music distribution. This required a seamless and interactive flow to
enable them to input all the necessary assets needed to showcase their
talents.

An important factor that this product already exists gave us the edge in

create materials accessible to users and intending artists willing to
submit their music effortlessly.

(IDEATION)
